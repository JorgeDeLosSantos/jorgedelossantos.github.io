Info

The two instant centers I13 and I24 move as the fourbar linkage goes through a cycle of motion. I13 is always at the intersection of links 1 and 3, and I24 is always at the intersection of links 2 and 4.

Note that in the Working Model file the coordinates of  I13 and I24 are entered as functions instead of constants.
