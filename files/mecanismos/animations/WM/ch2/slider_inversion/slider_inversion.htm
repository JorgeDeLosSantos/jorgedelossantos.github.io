Info

The four links - crank (in green), coupler (in red), slider (in blue), and track (in black) - are successively fixed (shown with an anchor) in each of the inversion. In the slider-crank (shown in upper right corner), the slider block translates; in the lower left inversion, the slider block rotates; in the lower right inversion, the slider block is fixed; and in the upper right inversion, the slider block has complex motion.
