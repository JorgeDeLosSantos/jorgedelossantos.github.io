Info

This saw (in yellow) has reciprocating motion inside the arm (in green), driven by a rotating disk (in orange) with a coupler (in grey). This is a crank slider mechanism. The arm pivots at the center of the disk to feed the saw downward to cut the metal bar (in purple).
