Info

The wishbone suspension is a fourbar linkage. The upper control arm (in blue) and lower control arm (in light blue) connects the wheel assembly, to the frame (in red). 

To simulate a car drives over a speed bump on one side, a position actuator is placed under the right wheel with its length changing.

The second simulation file shows the same suspension with reaction force shown on the spring, the upper control arm (a two-force member) and the lower control arm.
