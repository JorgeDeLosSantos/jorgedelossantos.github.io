Info

A segment of the coupler curve is close to a straight-line segment for film advancement. The tip of the coupler (called film hook) is inserted into the perforation of the film at an angle of approximately 90° to pull the film for advancing one frame. the hook leaves the film strip also at an angle of approximately 90°.
