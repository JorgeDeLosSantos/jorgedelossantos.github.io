Info

The walking beam (in green) is used in combination with a “pick-and-place” mechanism (in red and beige). The walking beam pushes articles (in yellow)  forward, and the material is caught in the pick-and-place mechanism. The sixbar walking beam allows moving more then one article, as compared to a fourbar walking beam.
